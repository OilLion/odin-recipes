Rice and Vegetable Meal Prep

Healthy and easy meal prep!

Ingredients

for approximately 6 servings

  • 2 medium-sized heads of broccoli
  • 2 bell pepper
  • many mushrooms
  • 4 large carrots
  • 500g rice
  • 350g soy cream
  • Two jalapeno peppers
  • Two red onions
  • Three or four cloves of garlic
  • Salt and spices to your liking
  • Butter, lime-seed oil or another flavour neutral frying agent

Instructions

Tipp: cook the rice, while you are preparing the rest of the food for cooking

  1. Cut the carrots and broccoli into pieces of manageable size, steam cook them in a large pot and add salt halfway through and stir
  2. Crush the garlic and cut it into small pieces. Cut the onions and fry both in a pan
  3. Add mushrooms
  4. When mushrooms are brown gold in colour, add soy cream and the bell peppers and let cook
  5. When almost cooked, add the jalapenos and spice until satisfied
  6. Fill 6 containers evenly and store in the fridge
