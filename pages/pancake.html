Pancakes

The perfect snack, fast and simple

Ingredients

For 4-5 large pancakes:

  • 2 large eggs
  • 200g flour
  • 200ml milk
  • 50g sugar
  • sweet toppings
  • margarine

Instructions

  1. Mix eggs, flour, sugar and milk in a large bowl. Stir until no more clumps. Add additional milk, until desired consistency is achieved - experiment
  2. Heat up a pan and coat it in margarine
  3. Pour the pancake mix into the pan, so that it evenly covers the base
  4. Fry until pancake has become golden brown from one side, then flip and repeat for the other side
  5. Add toppings of your choice

Comments

My personal topic recommendations are:

  • marmalade
  • apple sauce and cinnamon
  • Nutella

Savoury pancakes are also possible and delicious. Replace the sugar with salt and other spices to your liking. Cheese and bacon make excellent toppings for such a pancake
