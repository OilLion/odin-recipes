Pizza

A versatile delight, pizza can fit every occasion due the variety of toppings available

Ingredients

  • flour
  • water
  • olive oil
  • yeast
  • salt and garlic powder
  • tomato sauce
  • toppings of your choosing

Instructions

  1. Combine the correct amounts of flour and yeast in a large bowl. Toss in some salt and garlic powder
  2. Add the water and olive oil and stir until mixed properly
  3. Let the dough rise until it has risen
  4. Form the dough into the proper shape for a Pizza. You can now smear on the tomato sauce and add toppings to your liking

Comments
